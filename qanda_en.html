In First Place

Q. Why did you make this game?
Easy because I like to play games apart from what I liked about Seravy's style of play and his card system.

I also added in the game some Popularity Boom songs from Touhou 13.5 - Hopeless Masquerade to Reimu and Marisa where they must activate their card.
Q. Why are the decks default in Online?
Because the game was getting worse when you have different decks, and the best way to fix that was to put the default decks without modifying the offline ones...
Q. Why did you make the wikis?
So that people know how to play the game.

Basic Knowledge

Q. I don't understand what is 236B
The number in the word, such as "236B", indicates the address where the command is entered.
The address is expressed as if it were the numeric keypad layout as shown below.
A and B are button inputs only.

７８９ ＼↑／
４５６ ←Ｎ→
１２３ ／↓＼
 A＝Light or Long Attack,B＝Strong Attack,C＝Shooting or anything else,X＝Shoot A,Y＝Shoot B,Z=Other Things,D and W=Change the characters in Tag Team mode

In other words, if you type "236B", enter ↓\→
All you have to do is press the B button.

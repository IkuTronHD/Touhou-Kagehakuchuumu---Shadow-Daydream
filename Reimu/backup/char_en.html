Character: Reimu Hakurei

Character Features

Profile

The shrine maiden of the Hakurei Shrine.
She is a human who protects the border that divides the absurd and the reality.
She possesses an ability to fly in the air and exorcise evil spirits.

Whenever a incident occurs, he immediately rushes to resolve it, even though most of the time he feels like it's a nuisance.
However, she never manages to get rid of all the disturbances around her.
Rather, it may be the case that she attracts disturbances to herself.
